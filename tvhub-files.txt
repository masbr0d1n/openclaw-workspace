## рҹ”— API ENDPOINTS (TV Hub)

### Auth
- `POST /api/v1/auth/login`
- `POST /api/v1/auth/register`
- `POST /api/v1/auth/refresh`

### Videos
- `GET /api/videos`
- `POST /api/videos/upload`
- `GET /api/videos/[id]`
- `DELETE /api/videos/[id]`
- `GET /api/videos/file/[...path]`

### Channels
- `GET /api/channels`
- `POST /api/channels`
- `PUT /api/channels/[id]`
- `DELETE /api/channels/[id]`

### Playlists
- `GET /api/playlists`
- `POST /api/playlists`
- `PUT /api/playlists/[id]`
- `DELETE /api/playlists/[id]`

### Schedules (TV Guide)
- `GET /api/schedules`
- `POST /api/schedules`
- `PUT /api/schedules/[id]`
- `DELETE /api/schedules/[id]`

### Users
- `GET /api/v1/users`
- `POST /api/v1/users`
- `PUT /api/v1/users/[id]`
- `DELETE /api/v1/users/[id]`
